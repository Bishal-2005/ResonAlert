SMART VOICE SENSOR

Definition :

Advanced sensors that recognize specific voice patterns or keywords in real-time.

Technology:

Utilizes AI, machine learning, and natural language processing to detect distress signals.

FEATURES OF RESONALERT

Voice Recognition Technology

• Detects distress words like “help,” “stop,” or “no”.
• Recognizes unusual sound patterns indicating struggle or panic.

Real-Time Alert System

• Automatically sends alerts to pre-configured contacts or emergency services.
• Provides live location tracking and a continuous audio stream.

USE CASE AND IMPACT

Personal Safety

• Women’s safety against rape or assault.
• Children protection against bullying and kidnapping.

Public Safety

• Use in public places to detect and respond to accidents or assaults.
• Integration with public transportation systems to ensure passenger safety.
